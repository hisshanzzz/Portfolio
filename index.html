HEADLINE ACT · PYTHON · ZERO DEPENDENCIES · 24 UNIT TESTS

SpeechLens

I judge speech contests, and the #1 reason good speakers lose isn't content — it's timing and filler words. So I built SpeechLens: paste in a speech and it tells you if you'll finish inside the Toastmasters 5–7 minute window, which filler words you lean on, and how easy your sentences are to follow by ear. Pure Python, fully tested, built to coach my English Pro Hub students.

Price Trends in Sri Lanka

Every family feels prices rising, but the pattern is hard to see month by month. I analysed essential prices (rice, bread, milk, chicken, petrol, electricity, bus fare) from 2023–2024 with pandas and matplotlib — trend charts, percent change since January 2023, and written conclusions about affordability pressure.
